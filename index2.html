About Me

My career started 7 years ago as a copywriter for Leo Burnett Bulgaria where I was given a huge responsibility for a 21 years old - I was leading the whole ATL/BTL communication strategy for Diageo’s Johnnie Walker brand for three years. My favorite trophy from that time is Diageo’s Marketing Brilliance Award for Best Integrated Campaign Agency for 2012.

In 2013 I was invited to Benetton’s Fabrica Institute where I spent two weeks and contributed to Fabrica’s Colors magazine “Moving House” issue.

In 2014 I spent an year as a senior copywriter/general creative consultant at one of top 3 Bulgarian creative agencies - All Channels Communication Group, a FleishmanHillard Affiliate. I am proud of that period because I spent the year working really hard and the results proved it - we delivered successful campaigns for many clients and won a prestigious award at the Bulgarian Advertising Awards ceremony.

I spent an year in 2015 learning new things - Wordpress and Front end web development and consulting until I joined two childhood friends and we founded a game development company - Victus Games. For the first time in my career I was managing partner, head of marketing department, copywriter and graphic designer at the same time.

Running an independent game studio for two years teaches you a lot. I am almost sure I can write a book on how much I’ve learned about business, marketing, software development and life in the last three years. We’ve built a team of 10 people (2 of them were directly under me in Marketing dpt.), launched two betas and one Kickstarter campaign (failed), we built a community from scratch and learned hard lessons about business and life.

In early 2017 we launched the software development company SolvedOut, utilizing our talent, connections and knowledge and produced several software solutions for clients in Europe and USA. In late 2017 I couldn't resist the crypto hype and became interested in marketing for crypto projects. I advised an ICO and later I co-founded a small crypto consulting company.

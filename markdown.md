**Workout B: Lower Body & Core Focus**
- Squats: 3 sets x 6-8 reps
- Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets x 8-10 reps each leg

- **Stretching & Mobility:** 10 minutes of overall body stretches focusing on legs and core
